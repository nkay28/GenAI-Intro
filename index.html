The Art of Prompting

Context determines results. Moving from "Vague" to "Rich" context is the key leadership skill.

❌ The Vague Prompt

"Write me a briefing on the new policy."

Result: Generic output, no mission relevance, requires heavy editing.
✅ The Rich Context Prompt

"You are a policy analyst at [Agency]. Write a 1-page executive briefing for the Deputy Secretary on [Policy], focusing on workforce impact. Use formal tone and bullet points."

The 5 C's Framework

Character

Assign a specific role or persona.

Challenge

Define the specific task or problem.

Context

Provide background and audience info.

Constraints

Set word counts, tone, and limits.

Craft

Define the output format (Memo, Table, etc.)

Leader's Prompt Cheat Sheet

Copy and adapt these templates for immediate impact.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
"You are a senior policy analyst. Summarize the attached document in under 300 words for a Cabinet-level audience. Lead with the 3 most important findings. Include a risk/opportunity table."
DECISION BRIEF
"Act as a strategic advisor. I need to decide [DECISION]. Present 3 options with pros, cons, and a recommendation. Assume I care about mission impact, cost, and political feasibility."
MEETING PREP
"I have a meeting with [AUDIENCE] about [TOPIC]. Generate 5 questions I should ask, 3 key points I must make, and 2 anticipated objections with suggested responses."

30-Day Action Plan

Days 1–10: The Pilot Phase

Identify 2–3 routine tasks (emails, summaries, or research). Use an approved tool like Microsoft Copilot or Claude to generate first drafts.

Days 11–20: Mastery & Collaboration

Begin using the RACI (Role, Ask, Context, Instructions) model for complex tasks. Start a "Prompt Library" for your team to share what works.

Days 21–30: Scale & Review

Present findings to your leadership team. Review your agency's latest AI security guidance to ensure long-term compliance.

⚠️ Essential Guardrails

  • Verify Everything: AI can "hallucinate." Always fact-check citations and data.
  • Protect Data: Never input PII (Personally Identifiable Information) or classified data into non-secured tools.
  • Human Accountability: AI advises, but the Senior Executive owns the decision. You are the "Human in the Loop."
