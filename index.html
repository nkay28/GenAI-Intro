Generative AI: Using It Right

A practical, research-backed guide for leaders to use AI effectively, responsibly, and strategically.

What is Generative AI?

Generative AI creates content such as text, analysis, and summaries from natural language instructions. It is not a search engine or a human expert—it is a powerful assistant that amplifies your capabilities.

Reasoning & synthesis engine
24/7 analytical partner
Force multiplier for productivity

Context is Everything

❌ Vague Prompt

"Write me a briefing."

Result: Generic and not useful.

✅ Rich Prompt

"You are a policy analyst. Write a 1-page briefing for leadership with bullet points."

Result: Actionable and relevant.
